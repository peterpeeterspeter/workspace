### Content Formats That Work (From Competitor Analysis)

**1. "Best Sites" Listicles**
- Format: Ranked list with bonuses, key features, pros/cons
- Example titles: "Best Crash Casinos in 2026", "Top Crash Gambling Sites"
- Why works: High commercial intent, affiliate clicks
- Frequency: Updated annually/monthly

**2. Game-Specific Guides**
- Format: How to play, strategy, features, RTP info
- Examples: Aviator guides, crash game tutorials
- Why works: Captures players searching for specific games
- Key games: Aviator, Aviatrix, Balloon Run, Spaceman, JetX

**3. Strategy Content**
- Format: "Crash gambling strategy", "How to win", tips
- Examples: Fibonacci strategy, Martingale, bankroll management
- Why works: Players want to beat the game (even if they can't)
- Retention: High return visitor potential

**4. "How to Play" Tutorials**
- Format: Step-by-step guides with screenshots
- Why works: Beginner traffic, low competition
- Conversion: Demo ‚Üí real money funnel

**5. Software Provider Reviews**
- Format: Spribe, Turbo Games provider profiles
- Why works: B2B angle + player education
- Authority building: Shows expertise

**6. Regional/Geo-Specific Content**
- Format: "Best crash casinos for [country]", "Is crash gambling legal in [state]?"
- Why works: Lower competition, higher conversion
- Examples: China, Singapore, US states

**7. Provably Fair/Fairness Content**
- Format: "Is crash gambling rigged?", fairness audits
- Why works: Trust-building for skeptical players
- Differentiation: Most competitors don't cover this deeply
