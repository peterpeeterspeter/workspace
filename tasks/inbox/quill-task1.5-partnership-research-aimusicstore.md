## Research Questions

For each tool, answer:

**Tool Overview:**
- What does the tool do?
- Who is the target audience? (creators, producers, consumers)
- What's the pricing model? (free, freemium, paid)

**Contact Information:**
- Website: [URL]
- Partnership/biz dev email: [find "partnerships", "business development", "contact"]
- Social media: [Twitter/X, LinkedIn]
- Founders/key people: [if visible on site]

**Affiliate Program:**
- Do they have an affiliate program? [Yes/No]
- If Yes: What platform? (ShareASale, CJ, in-house?)
- If Yes: What commission? [% or $ amount]
- If Yes: How to sign up? [Link]

**Partnership Value:**
- Why would they want to partner with aimusicstore?
  - Free exposure to AI music creators?
  - "aimusicstore ranked" badge for social proof?
  - Traffic from highly-motivated users (voters)?
- What's our unique value proposition?

**Partnership Pitch:**
- What's the best angle?
- Example: "Add your tool to aimusicstore + get ranked by community + 'aimusicstore ranked' badge for your site + free traffic from highly-engaged AI music creators"

**Recommendation:**
- Priority: [HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW]
- Why? [Audience fit, traffic potential, brand alignment]
- Should we reach out first or wait until we have traction?
