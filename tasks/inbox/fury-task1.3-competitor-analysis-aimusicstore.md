## Research Questions

For each competitor, answer:

**Traffic & Audience:**
- Where does their traffic come from? (search, social, direct, referral)
- What's their estimated monthly traffic? (use SimilarWeb if accessible)
- Who is their primary audience? (creators, listeners, tool evaluators)

**Content Strategy:**
- What content do they produce? (blog posts, videos, social posts)
- How often do they publish?
- What content formats work best for them?

**Ranking Methodology:**
- How do they determine rankings? (votes, views, downloads, reviews?)
- Is their system transparent or opaque?
- Can rankings be gamed? How?

**Strengths:**
- What do they do well?
- What makes users trust them?
- What's their competitive advantage?

**Weaknesses:**
- What don't they do well?
- What frustrates users about them?
- What's missing from their approach?

**Our Differentiation:**
- How can aimusicstore be better?
- What unique value can we offer?
- What gap can we fill?
