## Description

Research and identify 10 AI music influencers (YouTubers and Twitter accounts) who could help promote aimusicstore. Focus on those who review AI music tools like Suno, Udio, Mubert, etc.

## Deliverables

**10 influencer profiles**, each including:
- Name/handle
- Platform (YouTube/Twitter)
- Follower count
- Engagement rate (likes, comments relative to followers)
- Contact information (email, DM, business contact)
- Content focus (what they cover)
- Best 5 to target for partnerships (ranked)
- Pitch angle for each

## Target Influencer Criteria

**YouTube Criteria:**
- 1K-100K subscribers (sweet spot: 5K-50K)
- Content about AI music tools (Suno, Udio, Mubert, Soundraw, Boomy)
- Active in last 30 days (recent upload)
- Good engagement (comments, likes)

**Twitter/X Criteria:**
- 5K-50K followers
- Tweets about AI music, AI tools, music production
- Active (tweets daily or weekly)
- Good engagement (replies, retweets)

## Influencer Types to Target

1. **AI Music Tool Reviewers** - Review Suno, Udio, other AI music tools
2. **AI Music Creators** - Share AI-generated tracks, discuss tools
3. **Music Production Tech** - Cover AI tools for producers
4. **AI/Generative Art** - Broader AI coverage but include music

## Research Process

**YouTube Search Queries:**
- "Suno AI review"
- "Udio tutorial"
- "AI music tools 2026"
- "best AI music generator"
- "Mubert AI music review"

**Twitter/X Search:**
- Search: "Suno AI", "Udio", "AI music tools"
- Look for accounts with 5K-50K followers
- Check recent engagement (replies, likes)
