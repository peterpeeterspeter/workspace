## Analysis Requirements

### 1. Content Strategy Analysis
For each site, analyze:
- Content types (guides, reviews, comparisons, news)
- Publishing frequency
- Content depth (average word count)
- Content freshness (last updated dates)
- Topical focus areas

**Use:** `web_fetch` to extract and analyze homepage + top pages

### 2. Keyword Targeting
Identify:
- Primary keywords they're targeting
- Keyword overlap with our sites
- Keywords they're winning at
- Gaps in their keyword coverage

### 3. Technical SEO Observations
Check:
- Site structure (URL patterns, hierarchy)
- Internal linking (how pages connect)
- On-page optimization (title tags, meta descriptions, headings)
- Page load indicators (from web_fetch performance)
- Mobile optimization clues

### 4. Strengths & Weaknesses
For each site, document:
**Strengths:**
- What they're doing well
- Why they're ranking
- Competitive advantages

**Weaknesses:**
- What they're missing
- Areas where we can beat them
- Technical issues
- Content gaps

### 5. Opportunity Matrix
Identify:
- **Quick wins:** Easy opportunities to outrank
- **Long-term plays:** Strategic advantages we can build
- **Niche gaps:** Untapped subtopics
- **Content improvements:** Better versions of existing content
