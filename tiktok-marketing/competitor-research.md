## What's Working on TikTok (2025-2026)

### 1. Fashion Brand Success Patterns

**Top Viral Strategies:**
- ✅ **Before/After Transformations** (highest performing)
- ✅ **"How I..." Storytelling** (authentic founder stories)
- ✅ **Behind the Scenes** (manufacturing, design process)
- ✅ **Money-Saving Hacks** (replace expensive services)
- ✅ **Time-Lapse Content** (show creation in seconds)
- ✅ **Trend Jacking** (using trending audio + your product)

**Key Insights from Research:**
- SKIMS treats every platform like a TV channel (massive investment)
- Luxury brands (Augustinus Bader) dominate with "immediate visible effects"
- Micro-influencers get **8.2% engagement** vs 5.3% for macro-influencers
- AI-generated UGC videos are **semi-viral** (new trend)

### 2. Hook Formulas That Work

**The Top 3 Viral Hook Patterns:**

**Pattern 1: "Replace Expensive Thing"**
- "I replaced my $5,000/month photographer with AI"
- "We stopped doing fashion shoots. Here's what happened."
- "Small brands: You don't need a photographer anymore."

**Pattern 2: "Time Reveal"**
- "From one photo to 20 product images in 30 seconds."
- "Watch AI create our entire catalog in real-time."
- "POV: You just realized you can skip photoshoots."

**Pattern 3: "Before/After Shock"**
- "This is what our product photos looked like..."
- "Then we tried AI. The difference is insane."
- "Same product, 10x better photos, 1/100th the cost."

### 3. AI Tool Marketing Trends

**What's Working for AI Tools on TikTok:**
- ✅ **Live Demos** (show the tool working on screen)
- ✅ **Speed Comparisons** (AI vs manual: "Watch this race")
- ✅ **Quality Comparisons** (AI output vs professional work)
- ✅ **Cost Breakdowns** ("This costs $5K. This costs $50.")
- ✅ **Narrative Arcs** (struggling founder discovers AI, transforms business)

**What's NOT Working:**
- ❌ Generic "use AI" content (too vague)
- ❌ Tech-focused explanations (TikTok wants RESULTS)
- ❌ Long intros (hook in first 1 second)

## TikTok Algorithm Insights (2025-2026)

### What Drives Virality

**Technical Factors:**
- **Watch time:** Keep viewers watching past 3 seconds
- **Engagement:** Comments, shares, saves matter more than likes
- **Sound:** Trending audio boosts reach significantly
- **Posting schedule:** 7:30 AM, 4:30 PM, 9 PM (user's timezone) work best
- **Consistency:** 3-5 posts/day max for accounts under 100K followers

**Content Factors:**
- **Authenticity:** Raw, unpolished often outperforms high-production
- **Relatability:** "I was struggling with X too" creates connection
- **Utility:** "Save this for later" content (tutorials, hacks, tips)
- **Surprise:** Pattern interrupts (unexpected transformations work)

### Fashion/Tech Intersection

**Key Trend:** AI + Fashion is growing but still early
- AI photo filters: Very saturated (consumers playing)
- AI tools for business: Underrepresented (opportunity)
- Fashion/ecommerce content: Oversaturated (but proven to work)

**Sweet Spot:** Photostudio.io sits at the intersection
- Fashion content (proven demand)
- AI tools (growing interest)
- Business utility (SMB pain point)

## Music & Trend Strategy

**Music is CRITICAL on TikTok:**
- **Always add trending music** from TikTok's library (30 seconds per post)
- **Don't use silent slideshows** (they get buried)
- **Match music energy to content energy** (exciting = upbeat, dramatic = cinematic)

**How to Find Trending Sounds:**
1. Open TikTok
2. Scroll For You page
3. Note tracks with "↑ rising" or viral badges
4. Save sounds that match your brand energy
5. Use within 48 hours (trends move fast)
