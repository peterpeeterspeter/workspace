## Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Money Savings (30%)
**Focus:** Replace expensive photoshoots
**Hooks:**
- "We replaced our $5,000/month photographer with AI. Here's what happened."
- "Small fashion brands: Stop paying for photoshoots. Here's the alternative."
- "From $5K/shoot to $0. This AI changed our business."
- "I spent $50K on product photography last year. Then I found this."

### Pillar 2: Before/After Transformation (40%)
**Focus:** Visual proof of quality
**Hooks:**
- "This is what our product photos looked like... then we tried AI."
- "Same product. One costs $5K to photograph. The other? $5."
- "Professional photographer vs AI. Can you tell the difference?"
- "Our catalog used to take 2 weeks to create. Now it takes 2 minutes."

### Pillar 3: Speed Demonstration (20%)
**Focus:** Real-time AI magic
**Hooks:**
- "Watch AI create our entire visual catalog in 30 seconds."
- "From one photo to 20 product images. Real-time demo."
- "Ghost mannequin. Flatlay. On-model. Video. All from one image."
- "I'm going to prove AI creates better visuals than your photographer. Watch."

### Pillar 4: SMB Education (10%)
**Focus:** Help small brands win
**Hooks:**
- "Small fashion brand hack: AI creates your entire catalog."
- "DTC brands: This is your competitive advantage."
- "Bootstrapping a fashion brand? Skip the photoshoots."

## Content Formats

### Format 1: Slideshow (Primary - 70%)
**Structure:**
1. Hook slide (text + before image)
2. Result slide (after image 1)
3. Result slide (after image 2)
4. Result slide (after image 3)
5. Result slide (after image 4)
6. CTA slide (after image 5 + URL)

**Duration:** 15-30 seconds total
**Music:** Trending upbeat track
**Text Overlays:** Minimal (let visuals speak)

### Format 2: Live Demo (Secondary - 20%)
**Structure:**
1. Hook slide
2-5. Screen recording of tool working
6. Final result

**Duration:** 30-45 seconds
**Music:** Electronic/tech vibes
**Text Overlays:** Step-by-step labels

### Format 3: Talking Head (Tertiary - 10%)
**Structure:**
1. Hook (face to camera)
2-4. Explain problem/solution
5. Show result
6. CTA

**Duration:** 45-60 seconds
**Music:** Soft background
**Text Overlays:** Key points only

## Weekly Testing Plan

### Week 1: Hook Category Testing
**Goal:** Identify which hook categories perform best

**Metrics to Track:**
- Views (first 24 hours)
- Watch time (completion rate)
- Engagement (likes, comments, shares, saves)
- CTR (if CTA included)

**What to Test:**
- Monday: Money savings vs Before/after
- Tuesday: Speed demo vs SMB education
- Wednesday: Mix best performers
- Thursday: Test variations of winners
- Friday: Final top 3 hooks

### Week 2: Format & Timing Testing
**Goal:** Optimize delivery

**What to Test:**
- Video length (15s vs 30s vs 60s)
- Posting times (morning vs afternoon vs evening)
- Music styles (upbeat vs cinematic vs trending)
- CTA timing (early vs late vs none)
