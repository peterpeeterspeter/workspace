## Critical Success Factors

### 1. Content Quality
- **Original research** - Don't regurgitate existing content
- **Practical value** - Actionable advice, specific details
- **Local expertise** - NL/BE specific information
- **Visual appeal** - Images, diagrams, tables

### 2. Internal Linking
- **Pillar → Cluster → Supporting** hierarchy
- **Related articles** at bottom of each page
- **Orphan pages** check (no page should be unlinked)
- **Anchor text** descriptive, not generic

### 3. Consistency
- **Publishing schedule** - 3-5 articles per week
- **Content updates** - Refresh top content quarterly
- **Monitoring** - Check rankings weekly

### 4. Patience
- **SEO takes time** - 3-6 months for initial rankings
- **Compounding effect** - Traffic grows exponentially
- **Authority builds** - Each article supports all others
