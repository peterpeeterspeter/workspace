## üìã Content Templates

### Pillar Page Structure (3,000 words)
```
H1: [Pillar] Complete Gids

Intro: Hook + What to expect (200 words)
Section 1: Overview (400 words)
Section 2: Types/Categories (600 words)
Section 3: Benefits/Why (400 words)
Section 4: How-To Guide (600 words)
Section 5: Resources/Directory (500 words)
Section 6: FAQ (300 words)

CTA: Email signup / Affiliate link
```

### Cluster Page Structure (1,200-2,000 words)
```
H1: [Specific Topic] Guide

Intro: Context + Promise (150 words)
Section 1: Deep dive (400 words)
Section 2: Examples/Case studies (300 words)
Section 3: Tips/Recommendations (300 words)
Section 4: Resources/Links (200 words)
FAQ (150 words)

CTA: Relevant offer
```

### Supporting Article (600-1,200 words)
```
H1: [Long-tail Question/Topic]

Intro: Direct answer (100 words)
Body: Explanation + Examples (400 words)
Conclusion: Summary + CTA (100 words)

Internal links: 2-3
External links: 1-2
```
