## ğŸ“ Key Concepts

### What is Topical Authority?

Topical authority is when search engines recognize your website as the **most comprehensive, trustworthy source** on a specific topic.

**How we build it:**
1. **Pillar Pages** - Broad overviews of each pillar
2. **Cluster Pages** - Specific sub-topics linking to pillar
3. **Supporting Articles** - Long-tail variations linking to clusters

**Result:** Google sees you as the expert â†’ You rank for everything in that topic.

### Why 4 Pillars?

Each pillar represents a **major user intent group** in the hobby niche:

1. **Hobbybeurzen** â†’ People looking for events to attend
2. **Workshops** â†’ People looking to learn/develop skills
3. **Creatieve Markten** â†’ People looking to buy handmade goods
4. **Hobbymaterialen** â†’ People looking to buy supplies

**Together:** They cover the entire hobby enthusiast journey from inspiration â†’ learning â†’ buying.

### Why This Strategy Works

1. **Low Competition First** - Start with easy wins, build momentum
2. **Content Clusters** - Internal linking boosts all articles
3. **User Intent Focus** - Each piece serves a specific user need
4. **Monetization Built-In** - Revenue streams mapped from day 1
5. **Scalable** - System can expand indefinitely
