## Validation Process

### Step 1: Run DataForSEO Research

For each keyword, collect:
- ✅ Search Volume (monthly)
- ✅ Keyword Difficulty (0-100)
- ✅ CPC (cost-per-click)
- ✅ Competition (0-1)
- ✅ Search Intent (informational, navigational, commercial, transactional)
- ✅ Trend (12-month history if available)

**Script to use:**

### Step 2: Compare Estimates vs Actual

For each keyword, record variance:

| Keyword | Est. Vol | Actual Vol | Variance | Est. KD | Actual KD | Variance | Action |
|---------|----------|------------|----------|---------|-----------|----------|--------|
| hobbybeurs nederland | 890 | TBD | TBD | 25 | TBD | TBD | TBD |

**Variance Thresholds:**
- **Volume:** Accept if within ±30% of estimate
- **Difficulty:** Accept if within ±5 points of estimate
- **If outside threshold:** Flag for re-prioritization

### Step 3: Adjust Strategy Based on Actuals

**If volume is 30%+ higher than estimated:**
- ✅ Increase priority
- ✅ Add more long-tail variations
- ✅ Consider earlier publishing

**If volume is 30%+ lower than estimated:**
- ⚠️ Decrease priority
- ⚠️ Consider merging with another keyword
- ⚠️ Skip if too low

**If difficulty is 5+ points higher than estimated:**
- ⚠️ Re-prioritize to later phase
- ⚠️ Focus on long-tail variations instead
- ⚠️ Increase content quality requirements

**If difficulty is 5+ points lower than estimated:**
- ✅ Increase priority (quick win!)
- ✅ Move to earlier phase
- ✅ Expand cluster with more articles

## Decision Matrix After Validation

### For Each Keyword, Ask:

1. **Is the search volume sufficient?**
   - Primary (500+/mo): ✅ Proceed
   - Secondary (100+/mo): ✅ Proceed
   - Long-tail (50+/mo): ✅ Proceed
   - Below 50/mo: ⚠️ Deprioritize or skip

2. **Is the difficulty achievable?**
   - KD < 30: ✅ Easy win, prioritize
   - KD 30-40: ⚠️ Medium difficulty, Phase 2
   - KD 40+: ⚠️ Hard, Phase 3 or skip
   - KD unknown: ⚠️ Flag for monitoring

3. **Is the intent right for our goals?**
   - Commercial: ✅ Good for revenue
   - Informational: ✅ Good for authority
   - Navigational: ⚠️ Lower priority
   - Transactional: ✅ Best for revenue

4. **Does this support our pillar strategy?**
   - Directly supports pillar: ✅ High priority
   - Supports cluster: ✅ Medium priority
   - Long-tail variation: ✅ Lower priority
   - Unrelated: ❌ Skip
