# DOMAIN EVALUATION FRAMEWORK
# Created: 2026-02-20

## SCORING METHODOLOGY (0-10 points each)

### 1. BRANDABILITY (0-10)
- **10:** Perfect brand name, 2 syllables, easy to spell/say
- **7-9:** Good brand, slight length/complexity issues
- **4-6:** Usable but not memorable
- **0-3:** Difficult to brand, too long, awkward

### 2. COMMERCIAL INTENT (0-10)
- **10:** High-value niche (insurance, legal, finance, SaaS)
- **7-9:** Good commercial value (e-commerce, services)
- **4-6:** Moderate value (blogs, info sites)
- **0-3:** Low/no monetization potential

### 3. KEYWORD VALUE (0-10)
- **10:** Exact match high-CPC keyword ($10+ CPC)
- **7-9:** Partial match good keyword ($5-10 CPC)
- **4-6:** Related to good keywords
- **0-3:** No keyword value

### 4. EXTENSION PENALTY (0-10)
- **10:** .com (no penalty)
- **8:** .net/.org in tech/nonprofit
- **5:** Country codes in target market
- **2:** Poor extension for use case

### 5. MARKET SIZE (0-10)
- **10:** $10B+ market
- **7-9:** $1-10B market
- **4-6:** $100M-1B market
- **0-3:** <$100M market

### 6. COMPETITION LEVEL (0-10)
- **10:** Low competition, easy to rank
- **7-9:** Moderate competition
- **4-6:** Competitive but possible
- **0-3:** Extremely competitive (SEO KD 80+)

### 7. LENGTH/CLARITY (0-10)
- **10:** 6-13 characters, clear meaning
- **7-9:** 14-18 characters, clear
- **4-6:** 19-25 characters or slightly unclear
- **0-3:** 25+ characters or confusing

### 8. DEVELOPMENT POTENTIAL (0-10)
- **10:** Can build $10k+/month business
- **7-9:** $1-10k/month potential
- **4-6:** $100-1k/month potential
- **0-3:** < $100/month or not developable

## TOTAL SCORE: 0-80

### TIER CLASSIFICATION
- **TIER 1 (70-80):** Excellent - Bid up to $2,500
- **TIER 2 (60-69):** Very Good - Bid up to $1,000
- **TIER 3 (50-59):** Good - Bid up to $500
- **TIER 4 (40-49):** Moderate - Bid up to $250
- **TIER 5 (<40):** Pass or <$100

## IMMEDIATE FILTERS (REJECT IF ANY TRUE)
- [ ] Trademark issues (major brands)
- [ ] Hyphenated domains
- [ ] Numbers in domain (unless meaningful)
- [ ] Over 25 characters
- [ ] Adult/gambling (unless intent match)
- [ ] Misspellings/typos
- [ ] Foreign words in wrong market
- [ ] Confusing spelling
- [ ] Negative words

## RESEARCH PRIORITY QUEUE

### BATCH 1: QUICK WINS (High scoring obvious ones)
Look for: 2-3 syllable .com, clear commercial intent, good CPC

### BATCH 2: NICHE PLAYS
Solid markets, moderate brandability

### BATCH 3: DEVELOPMENT PLAYS
Good for building out, not for parking

### BATCH 4: PASS
Low scores across all metrics
