## Part 1: Preparing Your Input Image

### What Makes a Good Input Photo?

AI can work wonders, but quality input matters. Here's what to aim for:

**Ideal Conditions:**
- **Good lighting:** Natural daylight or soft studio lighting (avoid harsh shadows)
- **Clean background:** Plain white or neutral background works best
- **Full product visible:** Entire garment or product in frame
- **Sharp focus:** No blur, camera shake, or motion blur
- **Accurate colors:** Represent true product colors

**Acceptable Input:**
- Smartphone photos (iPhone 12+ or equivalent recommended)
- Basic studio shots on mannequins
- Flatlay photos on tables or floors
- Existing catalog images (even lower quality ones)

**Equipment Needed:**
- Smartphone with decent camera (iPhone 13/14/15, Samsung S23/S24, Pixel 7/8)
- Or: Basic DSLR/mirrorless camera
- Natural window light or simple lighting setup
- Clean surface or background

### Shooting Techniques for Best Results

**On Garment/Hanger:**
1. Hang garment on a sturdy hanger
2. Use natural light from window (avoid direct sunlight)
3. Ensure garment is fully visible and not wrinkled
4. Shoot from straight-on angle

**On Table/Flat Surface:**
1. Lay garment flat, smooth out wrinkles
2. Use white or neutral background
3. Shoot from directly above (bird's eye view)
4. Ensure even lighting (avoid shadows)

**On Mannequin:**
1. Dress mannequin smoothly
2. Ensure proper fit and shape
3. Light from multiple angles to avoid harsh shadows
4. Capture full garment including hem

### Pro Tips: Input Photo Checklist

✅ Entire product visible in frame
✅ No significant blur or focus issues
✅ Colors appear accurate (not washed out or oversaturated)
✅ Reasonable lighting (not too dark, not blown out)
✅ Minimal background clutter (AI can remove, but cleaner helps)

❌ Avoid: Extreme backlighting, heavy shadows, motion blur, extreme angles

## Part 3: The AI Processing Workflow

### Step 1: Upload Your Image

Most platforms work similarly:

1. **Create account** on your chosen platform
2. **Navigate to upload** or "New Project"
3. **Select your input image** from your device
4. **Choose output type(s):** Ghost mannequin, flatlay, on-model, etc.

**Pro Tip:** Upload multiple angles (front, back, detail) at once for complete product coverage.

### Step 2: Configure Settings

Each platform has different options, but common settings include:

**Background:**
- Pure white (for marketplaces like Amazon, eBay)
- Transparent PNG (for design work)
- Custom color (match your brand)
- Lifestyle background (contextual scenes)

**Output Specifications:**
- Resolution: 2000x2000px (standard e-commerce)
- File format: JPG (photos), PNG (transparency needed)
- Quality: 80-100% (high enough for zoom)

**Styling Options:**
- Shadows: Natural, dramatic, or none
- Mannequin shape: Slim, regular, or full
- Fabric enhancement: Subtle, medium, or heavy

### Step 3: Generate and Review

Processing typically takes 10-60 seconds per image.

**What to Check:**
- ✅ Accurate shape and drape of garment
- ✅ Clean edges (no jagged lines or artifacts)
- ✅ Realistic shadows and lighting
- ✅ Accurate colors (compared to input)
- ✅ Clean background removal

**If Quality Issues:**
- Retouch using platform's editing tools
- Regenerate with different settings
- Try a different input photo if available
- Contact support if persistent issues

### Step 4: Download and Organize

**Download Formats:**
- **JPG:** For e-commerce platforms (smaller file size)
- **PNG:** If transparency needed (design work, overlays)
- **ZIP:** Multiple outputs bundled together

**File Naming Convention:**
```
SKU-[product-name]-[output-type]-[angle].jpg

Example: TSH-001-cotton-tee-ghost-front.jpg
         TSH-001-cotton-tee-flatlay-top.jpg
         TSH-001-cotton-tee-on-model-front.jpg
```

**Folder Structure:**
```
/Products/
  /TSH-001/
    input/
      raw-photo-1.jpg
    outputs/
      ghost-mannequin/
        front.jpg
        back.jpg
      flatlay/
        top.jpg
        detail.jpg
      on-model/
        front-1.jpg
        front-2.jpg
```

## Part 4: Optimizing for E-commerce Platforms

### Platform-Specific Requirements

**Amazon:**
- Pure white background (RGB 255,255,255)
- Minimum 1000x1000px (2000x2000px recommended)
- JPG format, 100% quality
- Product occupies 85%+ of frame

**Shopify:**
- PNG or JPG
- 2048x2048px or larger
- Consistent aspect ratio across all products
- Alt text for SEO

**eBay:**
- Maximum 12MB file size
- JPEG format preferred
- 1600x1600px recommended
- Supersize enabled (500x500px minimum)

**Your Own Store:**
- Match your site's design aesthetic
- Consider mobile optimization (vertical images)
- Enable zoom functionality (high resolution needed)
- Consistent cropping and alignment

### SEO Best Practices

**File Names:** Use descriptive, keyword-rich names
```
❌: IMG_1234.jpg
✅: mens-cotton-tshirt-ghost-mannequin-white.jpg
```

**Alt Text:** Describe image for accessibility and SEO
```
Good: "White cotton t-shirt ghost mannequin front view"
Poor: "tshirt.jpg" or "product photo"
```

**Image Sitemaps:** Submit to Google for indexing
```xml
<image:image>
  <image:loc>https://yourstore.com/products/tshirt-front.jpg</image:loc>
  <image:caption>White cotton t-shirt ghost mannequin</image:caption>
</image:image>
```

For <100 products:

2. Organize by SKU/product ID
3. Manually upload to your e-commerce platform
4. Add alt text and metadata
5. Publish/update products

### Bulk Import (Mid-Size Catalogs)

For 100-1000 products:
1. Export product catalog from your platform
2. Match image files to SKUs in spreadsheet
3. Use bulk import tools (Shopify CSV upload, etc.)
4. Review sample products before bulk publish

### API Integration (Large Catalogs)

For 1000+ products:

**Example: Shopify API**
```javascript

  images: [{
    src: "https://ai-platform.com/output/ghost-mannequin.jpg",
    alt: "Product name ghost mannequin front view",
    position: 1
  }]
});
```

3. Receive notification when images ready
4. Automatically update product in your store
5. Publish or schedule for release

## Part 8: Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Problem: Unnatural Shape

**Cause:** Poor input photo quality
**Solution:** Reshoot with better lighting and angle

### Problem: Color Inaccuracy

**Cause:** Input photo colors off or AI enhancement too strong
**Solution:** Reduce enhancement settings or use color correction tool

### Problem: Background Artifacts

**Cause:** Complex background or low resolution input
**Solution:** Use platform's touch-up tools or reshoot with cleaner background

### Problem: Slow Processing

**Cause:** High queue volume or large file sizes
**Solution:** Compress input images or process during off-peak hours
