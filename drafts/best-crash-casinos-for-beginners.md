## What Are Crash Games? (A Quick Primer for Complete Beginners)

Crash games are simple: you place a bet, watch a multiplier climb from 1.00x upward, and cash out before the game "crashes" (round ends). If you cash out at 2x, you double your bet. If you wait too long and the game crashes at 1.20x, you lose everything.

**Why crash is perfect for beginners:**
- **Simple rules:** Bet, watch multiplier, cash out. That's it.
- **Fast rounds:** Each game lasts 5-30 seconds. No complex strategies to learn.
- **Low minimums:** Many sites accept $0.10 bets. You can play 100 rounds on $10.
- **More control than slots:** YOU decide when to cash out. The game doesn't decide for you.

**How crash differs from slots:**
- Slots: Press spin, wait, win or lose. No control.
- Crash: Place bet, watch multiplier climb, click "cash out" whenever you want. **You're in control.**
