## Bonus Terms You Must Know

Before claiming any crash bonus code, understand the terms:

### Wagering Requirements (The Big One)

**What it is:** You must wager the bonus amount X times before withdrawing.

**Example:** $100 bonus with 30x wagering = $3,000 total bets required.

**How crash games contribute:**
- Most casinos: 100% (every dollar bet on crash counts toward wagering)
- Some casinos: 50% or 10% (read the terms — these are bad deals)
- **Our rule:** Only recommend bonuses where crash contributes 100%

### Game Restrictions

**Red flag:** "Bonus only valid on slots" or "Crash games excluded"

**What we list:** Only bonuses that work on crash games.

### Maximum Bet Limits

**What it is:** While playing with bonus money, you're capped on max bet size.

**Typical limits:** $5 per bet or 10% of bonus amount (whichever is lower)

**Why casinos do this:** Prevent bonus abuse (betting huge to clear wagering fast).

### Time Limits

**Typical:** 7-30 days to clear wagering

**Expired bonus:** If you don't clear wagering in time, bonus funds are forfeited.

**Our advice:** Don't claim bigger than you can clear. If you can only wager $500/week, don't claim a $5,000 bonus.
