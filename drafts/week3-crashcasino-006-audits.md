## Our 10-Point Casino Evaluation Framework

Every crash casino we review is scored on 10 criteria. Each criterion is weighted by importance (security and fairness matter most).

### 1. Licensing & Regulation (15% weight)

**What we check:**
- **License type:** MGA, UKGC, Curaçao (MGA/UKGC = highest score)
- **License verification:** We verify the license number on the regulator's website
- **License status:** Active, suspended, or expired
- **Regulatory oversight:** Does the regulator actually enforce rules?

**Red flags:**
- No license listed
- Fake license (doesn't exist on regulator's site)
- License from "banana republic" jurisdiction (no real regulation)

**Why it matters:** Licensed casinos are accountable. If they scam you, you can file a complaint. Unlicensed casinos can do whatever they want.

### 2. Security Features (20% weight)

**What we check:**
- **SSL encryption:** HTTPS, valid certificate, padlock in URL bar
- **2FA (Two-Factor Authentication):** Can you secure your account?
- **Cold storage:** Does the casino store funds offline (safer than hot wallets)?
- **Data protection:** GDPR compliance, privacy policy

**Red flags:**
- No SSL (HTTP only)
- No 2FA option
- All funds in hot wallets (vulnerable to hacks)
- Vague privacy policy

**Why it matters:** If a casino gets hacked, you could lose your deposits. Security is non-negotiable.

### 3. Game Fairness (25% weight) - **MOST IMPORTANT**

**What we check:**
- **Provably fair system:** Can you verify crash results independently?
- **RTP (Return to Player):** What's the house edge? (97%+ is good, 95% or less is bad)
- **Third-party audits:** eGaming, iTech Labs, GLI certification?
- **RNG certification:** Is the crash game RNG truly random?

**How we test:**
1. **Play 100+ rounds** and record results
2. **Verify provably fair** with the casino's tool
3. **Calculate actual RTP** from our results vs. advertised RTP
4. **Check for anomalies** (e.g., crash consistently hitting 1.00x)

**Red flags:**
- No provably fair system
- Can't verify results independently
- Actual RTP lower than advertised (rigged game)
- Crash suspiciously crashes at 1.00x or 1.01x repeatedly

**Why it matters:** If the game is rigged, nothing else matters. You can't win.

### 4. Crash Game Quality (10% weight)

**What we check:**
- **Crash game variety:** Original crash, Aviator, customized variants?
- **Graphics & UX:** Is the game smooth, lag-free, visually appealing?
- **Mobile compatibility:** Works on iOS/Android?
- **Speed:** Fast rounds, instant cashout registration?

**Red flags:**
- Laggy crash game (cashout delays)
- Poor mobile experience
- Only one crash game option
- Glitches or bugs

**Why it matters:** You'll be playing a lot. The game should be enjoyable.

### 5. Payment Methods & Speed (10% weight)

**What we check:**
- **Deposit options:** Crypto, credit card, bank transfer?
- **Withdrawal speed:** How long do payouts take? (Crypto: <1 hour, Fiat: <24 hours)
- **Withdrawal limits:** Min/max per day/week/month?
- **Fees:** Are withdrawal fees reasonable?

**How we test:**

**Red flags:**

**Why it matters:** If you can't withdraw your winnings, what's the point?

### 6. Bonuses & Wagering Requirements (5% weight)

**What we check:**

**Why it matters:** Bonuses should be a nice perk, not a trap. Most bonuses aren't worth claiming.
