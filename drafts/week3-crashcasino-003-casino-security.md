## 3. Provably Fair Crash Games

**The most important security feature for crash gambling.**

Crash games are prone to manipulation. A shady casino can rig the RNG (Random Number Generator) to crash at low multipliers more often than mathematically expected.

**Provably Fair** means you can verify each round's fairness using cryptographic hashes.

**How Provably Fair works:**
1. Casino generates a random seed (server seed)

**What to look for:**

## 4. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

**Prevents hackers from draining your account.**

2FA requires a second verification step (usually a code from your phone) when logging in or withdrawing. Even if a hacker steals your password, they can't access your account without the 2FA code.

**What to look for:**
- **Google Authenticator support** (best)
- **Authy or similar 2FA apps**
- **SMS 2FA** (less secure but better than nothing)

**âš ï¸ Red flag:** No 2FA option, or 2FA only for withdrawals (not login).

## How Scam Casinos Operate

**Understanding the enemy.**

**Common scam tactics:**

**1. Fake License:**
- Casino displays "Licensed by MGA" but the license number is fake or belongs to another casino
- **Check:** Verify the license number on the regulator's official website

**2. Rigged Crash Games:**
- Casino claims "Provably Fair" but the verification tool is fake or missing
- **Result:** You lose far more often than RTP suggests
- **Check:** Use the verification tool after every round

**3. Withdrawal Stalling:**
- Casino approves your deposit but "delays" your withdrawal for weeks
- **Excuse:** "Security check," "pending approval," "technical issues"
- **Reality:** Casino is broke or planning an exit scam
- **Check:** Withdrawal terms should state processing time clearly

**4. Hidden Fees:**
- Casino charges "withdrawal processing fees," "inactivity fees," or "account maintenance fees"
- **Check:** Read the fee schedule before depositing

**5. Bonus Trap:**
- Casino offers 200% deposit bonus but requires 50x wagering (impossible to clear)
- **Result:** Your deposit is locked until you lose it all
- **Check:** Read bonus T&Cs carefully (wagering requirements, game restrictions)
