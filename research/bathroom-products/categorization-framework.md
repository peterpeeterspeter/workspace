# Bathroom Product Categorization Framework

**Created:** 2026-02-12  
**Purpose:** Refined, granular categorization for DeBadkamer.com product catalog

---

## Style Categories (Primary)

### 1. Modern Minimalist
- **Characteristics:** Clean lines, simple shapes, uncluttered, neutral colors
- **Keywords:** sleek, contemporary, streamlined, geometric, understated
- **Price Range:** Budget to Mid-range
- **Target:** Urban professionals, small bathrooms, new builds

### 2. Classic Elegant
- **Characteristics:** Traditional shapes, ornate details, curved lines, timeless appeal
- **Keywords:** elegant, traditional, ornate, curved, vintage-inspired
- **Price Range:** Mid-range to Premium
- **Target:** Renovation projects, period homes, luxury apartments

### 3. Luxury Premium
- **Characteristics:** High-end materials, designer collaborations, advanced features, exceptional craftsmanship
- **Keywords:** designer, exclusive, bespoke, artisanal, statement pieces
- **Price Range:** Premium to Ultra-luxury
- **Target:** High-end residential, boutique hotels, architect projects

### 4. Budget Functional
- **Characteristics:** Simple, practical, durable, affordable, mass-produced
- **Keywords:** practical, durable, economical, essential, utility-focused
- **Price Range:** Budget
- **Target:** First-time buyers, rental properties, budget renovations

## Product Type Categories

### Bathtubs (Category: Bathtub)

**By Installation:**
- **Freestanding** (vrijstaand): Not attached to walls, can be placed anywhere
- **Back-to-Wall** (half vrijstaand): One side against wall, other sides exposed
- **Drop-in** (inbouw): Dropped into deck or platform
- **Corner** (hoekbad): Fits into corner, space-saving
- **Alcove** (niches): Three-sided, walled-in design
- **Oval** (ovaal): Rounded, freestanding design

**By Features:**
- **Whirlpool** (whirlpoolbad): Hydro massage jets, therapeutic
- **Air Bath** (luchtbad): Air jets, gentle massage
- **Combination** (combinatie): Hydro + air massage
- **Soaking** (ligbad): Simple, deep soaking tub
- **With LED Lighting** (met LED): Ambient lighting built-in
- **With Heater** (met verwarming): Maintains water temperature

**By Material:**
- **Acrylic** (acryl): Lightweight, warm to touch, affordable
- **Solid Surface** (solid surface): Seamless, durable, premium feel
- **Ceramic** (keramiek): Traditional, heavy, glossy finish
- **Cast Iron** (gietijzer): Vintage, heavy, excellent heat retention
- **Stone Resin** (steenhars): Natural look, warm feel, premium

**By Size:**
- **Small** (120-140cm): Compact, space-saving
- **Medium** (150-170cm): Standard size
- **Large** (170-190cm): Spacious, luxury
- **Extra Large** (190cm+): Oversized, statement piece

### Fixtures & Fittings (Category: Faucet)

**Waste Drains (Afvoer):**
- **Click Waste** (klikafvoer): Push-to-close drain
- **Pop-Up Waste** (pop-up afvoer): Spring-loaded drain cover
- **Grid Waste** (roosterafvoer): Slotted drain cover
- **Overflow** (overloop): Safety overflow drain

**Urinal Fixtures (Urinoir):**
- **Wall-Hung** (wandgemonteerd): Wall-mounted bowl
- **Floor-Standing** (vrijstaand): Floor-mounted
- **Rear-Spill** (achteruitlaat): Rear inlet/outlet

**Faucet Sets (Kranensets):**
- **Wall-Mounted** (wandgemonteerd): Wall installation
- **Deck-Mounted** (opvangmontage): Deck installation
- **Freestanding** (vrijstaand): Floor-standing filler

**Bathtub Fillers (Badvullers):**
- **Deck-Mounted** (badkraan op badrand): On tub rim
- **Freestanding** (vrijstaande badkraan): Floor-standing
- **Wall-Mounted** (wandgemonteerd): Wall installation

**Protection:**
- **Tub Covers** (beschermhoezen): Outdoor protection

### Toilets (Category: Toilet)

**By Installation:**
- **Wall-Hung** (wandcloset): Concealed cistern, floating bowl
- **Back-to-Wall** (hoek closet): Cistern visible, bowl close to wall
- **Floor-Standing** (staand closet): Traditional floor-mount
- **Corner** (hoektoilet): Space-saving corner installation

**By Features:**
- **Rimless** (zonder spoelrand): Hygienic, easy-clean design
- **Soft-Close** (softclose): Gentle seat close
- **Quick-Release** (quickrelease): Removable seat for cleaning
- **Integrated Bidet** (met bidet): Built-in bidet function
- **DirectFlush** (directflush): Rimless + powerful flush
- **TwistFlush** (twistflush): Dynamic flush technology

**By Design:**
- **Compact** (compact): Small footprint, space-saving
- **Oval** (ovaal): Rounded bowl shape
- **Square** (vierkant): Angular, contemporary
- **Round** (rond): Traditional round bowl

**By Color:**
- **White** (wit): Classic white
- **Almond/Beige** (almond, beige): Warm off-white
- **Stone White** (stone white): Soft off-white
- **Matte Black** (mat zwart): Dark, contemporary
- **Matte Grey** (mat grijs): Neutral, modern

### Lighting (Category: Lighting)

**By Type:**
- **LED Mirrors** (LED spiegel): Integrated lighting
- **Mirror Cabinets** (spiegelkast): Storage + mirror + lighting
- **Wall Lights** (wandlampen): Sconce-style fixtures
- **Ceiling Lights** (plafonlampen): Overhead fixtures

**By Features:**
- **Direct + Indirect** (direct en indirect): Dual lighting zones
- **Dimmable** (dimbaar): Adjustable brightness
- **Heating** (spiegelverwarming): Anti-fog heating
- **Color Changing** (kleurveranderend): RGB/ambient lighting
- **Touch Control** (touch bediening): Touch-sensitive controls
- **Remote Control** (afstandsbediening): Wireless operation

**By Finish:**
- **Chrome** (chroom): Polished chrome
- **Brushed Chrome** (geborsteld chroom): Satin finish
- **Copper PVD** (koper PVD): Physical vapor deposition
- **Matte Black** (mat zwart): Dark finish

### Tiles (Category: Tile)

**By Type:**
- **Ceramic** (keramiek): Classic, affordable
- **Porcelain** (porselein): Dense, durable, water-resistant
- **Natural Stone** (natuursteen): Marble, granite, travertine
- **Metro Tiles** (metrotegels): Subway-style rectangles
- **Mosaic** (mozaiek): Small tile patterns

**By Finish:**
- **Gloss** (glans): Shiny, reflective
- **Matte** (mat): Non-reflective
- **Honed** (gezoet): Smooth, matte
- **Polished** (gepolijst): High-gloss

**By Size:**
- **Small** (10x10cm - 20x20cm): Mosaic to small format
- **Medium** (30x30cm - 60x60cm): Standard format
- **Large** (60x60cm+): Large format, fewer grout lines
- **Extra Large** (120x120cm+): Extra large format

### Vanity Units (Category: Vanity)

**By Type:**
- **Basin Cabinet** (wastafelkast): Storage below sink
- **Floating Vanity** (hangend wastafelmeubel): Wall-mounted, space below
- **Floor-Standing** (vrijstaand wastafelmeubel): Floor-mounted unit
- **Double Vanity** (dubbel wastafel): Two sinks

**By Features:**
- **Integrated Basin** (geïntegreerde wastafel): One-piece design
- **Soft-Close Drawers** (softclose laden): Gentle close
- **Adjustable Shelves** (verstelbare planken): Configurable storage
- **LED Lighting** (met verlichting): Built-in lights

**By Material:**
- **Wood** (hout): Natural, warm feel
- **High-Gloss Lacquer** (hoogglans lak): Contemporary, glossy
- **Matte Lacquer** (mat lak): Modern, non-reflective
- **Textured** (getextureerd): Tactile finish

## Price Categories

### Budget (€80 - €500)
- Functional, essential products
- Mass-produced, standard designs
- Practical over decorative
- Target: First-time buyers, rentals

### Mid-Range (€500 - €1,500)
- Balance of style and value
- Better materials, more features
- Design-conscious, affordable luxury
- Target: Homeowners, renovation projects

### Premium (€1,500 - €3,000)
- Designer brands, superior materials
- Advanced features, premium finishes
- Exceptional craftsmanship
- Target: Luxury homes, architect projects

### Ultra-Luxury (€3,000+)
- Bespoke, exclusive designs
- Limited editions, statement pieces
- Artisanal craftsmanship
- Target: High-end residential, boutique hotels

## Target User Profiles

### 1. Urban Professionals (25-35 years)
- **Style:** Modern minimalist
- **Priorities:** Space-saving, clean design, easy maintenance
- **Budget:** Mid-range
- **Products:** Compact baths, wall-hung toilets, LED mirrors

### 2. Family Renovators (35-50 years)
- **Style:** Modern minimalist to classic elegant
- **Priorities:** Durability, safety, storage, comfort
- **Budget:** Mid-range to premium
- **Products:** Standard baths, back-to-wall toilets, vanity units

### 3. Luxury Homeowners (45-65 years)
- **Style:** Luxury premium
- **Priorities:** Quality, exclusivity, advanced features
- **Budget:** Premium to ultra-luxury
- **Products:** Freestanding baths, designer fixtures, custom cabinets

### 4. Commercial Projects
- **Style:** Modern minimalist to budget functional
- **Priorities:** Durability, compliance, low maintenance
- **Budget:** Budget to mid-range
- **Products:** Commercial toilets, urinals, durable fixtures

### 5. Interior Designers/Architects
- **Style:** All categories (project-specific)
- **Priorities:** Aesthetics, customization, brand reputation
- **Budget:** Mid-range to ultra-luxury
- **Products:** Statement pieces, designer collaborations, bespoke

## Search & Filter Attributes

### Primary Filters
1. **Product Category** (bathtub, toilet, faucet, lighting, tile, vanity)
2. **Style** (modern minimalist, classic elegant, luxury premium, budget functional)
3. **Price Range** (budget, mid-range, premium, ultra-luxury)
4. **Brand** (Riho, Villeroy & Boch, Geberit, etc.)
5. **Installation Type** (freestanding, wall-hung, back-to-wall, corner)

### Secondary Filters
6. **Material** (acrylic, solid surface, ceramic, brass, etc.)
7. **Finish Color** (white, matte white, stone white, black, etc.)
8. **Size** (small, medium, large, extra large)
9. **Features** (whirlpool, LED, soft-close, rimless, etc.)
10. **Application** (residential, commercial, outdoor)

### Advanced Filters
11. **Sustainability** (water-saving, eco-friendly materials)
12. **Certifications** (CE, ISO, WaterSense)
13. **Warranty** (standard 5 years, extended 10+ years)
14. **Made In** (EU, Germany, Netherlands, Belgium)
