## Refined Categorization Framework

### 4 Primary Style Categories

1. **Modern Minimalist** (27.4% of catalog)
   - Clean lines, simple shapes, uncluttered, neutral colors
   - Keywords: sleek, contemporary, streamlined, geometric, understated
   - Target: Urban professionals, small bathrooms, new builds

2. **Budget Functional** (44.6% of catalog)
   - Simple, practical, durable, affordable, mass-produced
   - Keywords: practical, durable, economical, essential, utility-focused
   - Target: First-time buyers, rental properties, budget renovations

3. **Premium** (23.4% of catalog)
   - Designer brands, superior materials, advanced features, exceptional craftsmanship
   - Keywords: designer, exclusive, bespoke, superior craftsmanship
   - Target: Luxury homes, architect projects, quality-focused renovators

4. **Luxury Premium** (4.6% of catalog)
   - High-end materials, designer collaborations, advanced features, statement pieces
   - Keywords: exclusive, bespoke, artisanal, statement, investment piece
   - Target: High-end residential, boutique hotels, architect projects
