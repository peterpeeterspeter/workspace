## Undergraduate Programs

The business world of tomorrow will be new and different from what is familiar today. To succeed, you must learn the skills for the jobs of the future, and you must understand how to innovate, collaborate, and communicate. At Mays Business School, our undergraduate programs will prepare you to succeed throughout your career. At the center of all our programs are the Aggie Core Values: excellence, integrity, leadership, loyalty, respect and selfless service. Come be inspired to change the world forever.

## Graduate Programs

As an MBA or master’s student, Mays Business School will help you advance your career and position you to become a leader in the world of business. Mays is a top-ranked business school that helps students to cultivate their talents while gaining access to an unmatched, worldwide network of former students. Come be amazed by the opportunities with industry-leading businesses across the world.
