## Legal & Compliance

**India-Specific Considerations:**

1. **State Laws:** Betting laws vary by state (some allow, some don't)
2. **Online Betting:** Grey area - most operators operate offshore
3. **Taxation:** Winnings may be taxable (30% + cess)
4. **Advertising:** Restrictions on gambling advertising

**Disclaimer Requirements:**
- Age restrictions (18+)
- Responsible gambling messaging
- No guaranteed winnings claims
- Clear affiliate relationship disclosure
