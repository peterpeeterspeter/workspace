## America's First Free Online Counseling Center for Adult Students

Founded in 1989 and online since 1998, GetEducated is America’s first free online counseling center for adult students. Our mission is to help consumers make informed choices about one of the most expensive purchases they will make in their lifetime—a college degree. We provide unique tools that help consumers review and compare more than 35,000 online degree programs.
