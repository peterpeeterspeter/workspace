## Key Findings

- **High CPC keywords** (betting = ₹80-200 per click)
- **Multiple revenue streams** (affiliate + premium membership + ads)
- **Growing Indian market** (online betting adoption rising)
- **Clear monetization path** (bookmaker affiliate programs)

### ⚠️ Cons
- **Competitive SERPs** (established players like inhorseracing.com, thetopbookies.com)
- **Content-intensive** (need 3-5 articles/week)
- **Regulatory uncertainty** (India's grey-area betting laws)
- **Slow initial growth** (12-18 months to profitability)
