## Special Conditions & Considerations

### Commission Structures

**Percentage-Based (Most Common):**
- Coursera: 15-45% of sale
- Amazon: Up to 10% (varies by category)
- edX: Not disclosed (likely percentage-based)

**Lead-Based (Less Common):**
- Some aggregators may pay per qualified lead
- University partnerships typically pay per enrollment inquiry
- Student loan providers often pay per funded loan

**Hybrid Models:**
- Some programs offer base commission + performance bonuses
- Coursera offers bonuses for strong performance

### Cookie Durations

**Standard Durations:**
- **30 days:** Coursera (excellent)
- **24 hours:** Amazon (short but high volume)
- **7-14 days:** Typical for many programs
- **Session-based:** Some aggregators use session tracking only

**Impact on Earnings:**
- Longer cookie = higher conversion rate
- Short cookie requires immediate action
- Consider promoting urgency to encourage faster clicks

### Attribution Models

**Last Click:** Most common (last affiliate to get credit)
**First Click:** Less common (first affiliate to get credit)
**Multi-Touch:** Rare but more fair (splits credit)

## Compliance & Best Practices

### FTC Disclosure Requirements

**Required Disclosures:**
- Clear and conspicuous disclosure of affiliate relationships
- Placement near affiliate links
- Language: "This post contains affiliate links" or similar
- Disclosure before first affiliate link

**Example Language:**
> "This article contains affiliate links. If you purchase through these links, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you."

### Other Legal Considerations

**Trademark Usage:**
- Use proper trademark symbols (Â®, â„¢)
- Don't imply official partnership unless true
- Follow each program's branding guidelines

**False Claims:**
- Only make truthful, substantiated claims
- Don't promise guaranteed results
- Disclose limitations where appropriate

**Data Privacy:**
