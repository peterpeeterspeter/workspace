## Writing Notes for Content Writer

### Important Tips for Writing This Content

1. **Start Simple:** Assume reader has NEVER played a casino game before. Explain "multiplier" like you're explaining to a 12-year-old.

2. **Use Examples:** Instead of "the multiplier increases," say "if you bet $1 and cash out at 2x, you win $2."

3. **Be Encouraging, Not Scary:** Focus on learning and fun, not "you'll lose money." Emphasize demo practice and low stakes.

4. **Add Personal Touch:** Use "we" when sharing experience ("We've tested 50+ casinos," not "The author has tested..."). Build connection.

5. **Keep Sections Short:** Beginners skim. Use bullet points, numbered lists, bold text. Avoid 500-word paragraphs.

6. **Repeat Key Points:** Mention "demo mode" and "minimum bets" at least 5 times. Beginners need repetition.

7. **Use Analogies:** Compare crash to stock market ("invest, watch it climb, sell before crash"). Makes it relatable.

8. **Include Specifics:** Don't say "low minimum bets." Say "$0.10 minimum bets at Cybet." Specifics build trust.

9. **Add Warnings (Gently):** Mention risks without fear-mongering. "Crash games are volatile" not "You'll lose everything."

10. **End with Confidence:** Conclusion should make reader feel READY to play, not scared to try.

---

### Things to Avoid

1. **NO Outcome Promises:** Don't say "You'll win," "Guaranteed profits," "Beat the house."
   - **Right:** "You can learn the game"
   - **Wrong:** "You can make money"

2. **NO Jargon Without Explanation:** Don't say "provably fair" without explaining it.
   - **Right:** "Provably fair means every round can be verified as random."
   - **Wrong:** "All sites use provably fair." (Assumes knowledge)

3. **NO Affiliate Hype:** Don't say "Best casino ever!" Explain WHY it's best for beginners.
   - **Right:** "$0.10 minimums mean you can play 100 rounds on $10."
   - **Wrong:** "Unbeatable bonuses!" (Generic)

4. **NO Pushy CTAs:** Don't spam "JOIN NOW!" every paragraph. One clear CTA in conclusion + natural mentions.

5. **NO Overwhelming Tables:** Don't list 20 casinos. Stick to top 5. Beginners get decision paralysis.

6. **NO Complex Strategies:** Don't explain Kelly Criterion or Fibonacci. Keep it simple: auto-cashout at 1.5x-2x.

7. **NO Fear-Mongering:** Don't say "Most beginners lose everything." Say "Set limits to manage risk."
