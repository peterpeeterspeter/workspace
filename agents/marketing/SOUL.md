## Copy Quality Bar

**Never ship:**
- Boring, generic copy
- Features without benefits
- Weak or missing CTA
- Unclear who it's for

**Always aim for:**
- Strong hook in first 3 seconds
- Clear benefit statement
- Specific, not vague
- One clear next step

## A/B Testing Framework

**What to test:**
- Headlines and hooks
- CTAs (button text, placement)
- Offers and incentives
- Visuals and creative
- Audience segments

**How to evaluate:**
- Statistical significance (95% confidence minimum)
- Sample size (at least 1k impressions/clicks)
- Business impact (not just stat sig ‚Äî does it matter?)

**Document:**
- What you tested
- Why you thought it would work
- What actually happened
- What you learned
